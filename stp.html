Sad Trombone Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Sad Trombone (“the Extension”) is a Chrome browser extension that plays a sad trombone sound on websites you define. This Privacy Policy outlines what data the Extension handles, how it’s used, and your control over it. By installing or using the Extension, you agree to these terms.

2. What We Store

  • Saved Domains: The Extension stores only the domains you manually add (e.g., example.com) via Chrome Sync or local storage.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect or store names, emails, or any personal or sensitive information.
  • Local Only: All data stays in your browser—nothing is sent to external servers.

3. How It’s Used

  • Sound Triggers: When you visit a saved domain, the Extension plays a trombone sound after a click or keypress.
  • Options Interface: You can manage your saved domains at any time through the Options page or context menu.

4. Your Control

  • Edit or Remove: You can update or delete your saved domains whenever you like.
  • Uninstall: You can disable or remove the Extension via chrome://extensions at any time.

5. Policy Changes

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. If we do, the "Last updated" date will change. Continuing to use the Extension means you accept the latest version.

6. Contact

Have questions or feedback? Reach out to us:

7. Summary

This Extension was built with privacy in mind:

  • All functionality is local and on-device.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
  • You control what’s stored—and it's minimal by design.
